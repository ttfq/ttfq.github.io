Google Chrome
1. Go to Settings.
2. In the Privacy and security section, click on Security.
3. In the Advanced section, enable Use secure DNS.
4. Select With: Custom, then enter https://dns.nextdns.io/ba4232.

Firefox
Windows, macOS and Linux only
1. Open Preferences.
2. Scroll down to the Network Settings section and click on Settings.
3. Scroll down and check Enable DNS over HTTPS.
4. Select Custom, enter https://dns.nextdns.io/ba4232 and click OK.
5. Enter "about:config" in the address bar (and click on I Accept the risk! if asked).
6. Set network.trr.mode to 3.
